Durham, NC

Agenda

  • What is the goal of the curriculum?
  • Who is the curriculum aimed at?
  • What is in the curriculum?
  • What is the Global Growth app?
  • What do I need to do before April 4?

Goal

To leave students with enough knowledge that they could go on to learn Clojure on their own.

Audience assumptions

  • Smart
  • Motivated
  • No development experience

Curriculum contents

  • Numbers, strings, booleans
  • Vectors and maps
  • Functions
  • if and boolean logic
  • Setting up a Clojure project
  • Web development basics with Ring
  • Deploying to Heroku

Is this ambitious?

YUP

The Global Growth app

A web application that uses the World Bank API to show lists of countries by economic/development factor.
This application will be 80% baked, with 20% for students to fill in.

Global Growth demo

How to prepare

  • Read the curriculum
  • Set up Light Table and get familiar with it
  • Try downloading and filling in the Global Growth app
  • Try deploying the app to Heroku
